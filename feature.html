Welcome to NM BARRIOS Restaurant

EAT ALL YOU CAN!

NM Barrios Restaurant

Experience the best of Filipino cuisine at NM Barrios Restaurant, a beloved buffet spot in Camachiles, Mabalacat City, Pampanga. We offer an unlimited feast of local favorites, from sizzling sisig to hearty kare-kare — all freshly prepared and served with genuine Kapampangan hospitality. Whether you're celebrating with family or just craving a satisfying meal, NM Barrios is your destination for indulgence and tradition.

Interior of the Restaurant

Our spacious buffet hall is designed for comfort and celebration. With clean lines, warm lighting, and ample seating, NM Barrios creates the perfect setting for gatherings of all sizes. The layout encourages easy access to our buffet stations while maintaining a cozy, welcoming vibe that reflects the heart of Pampanga.
